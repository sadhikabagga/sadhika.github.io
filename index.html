Hello, and welcome to my website!

I am a sixth year Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of Texas at Austin. My primary areas of interest are in macroeconomics and labor economics.

My current research focuses on understanding the link between the decline in labor market dynamism and increasing market power of firms.

For more information please contact me at: sbagga@utexas.edu or sbagga1020@gmail.com.

I will be a Postdoctoral Fellow at Columbia University in 2023-24 and join the Department of Economics at the University of Rochester as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2024.
